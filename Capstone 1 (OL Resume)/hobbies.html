My Hobbies

Music Production

My favorite, most time consuming hobby is producing music using a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) called FL Studio. While not advanced by any means, I have been obsessed with learning how to create unique synthesizer sounds and arrange Electronic Dance Music (EDM) since I started in 2020. If you're brave enough to endure it, you can find some of my work on SoundCloud.com under the name Lankorde.

Home Improvement

When I'm not producing music, I'm usually finding something in my house to work on. Nothing as large-scale as renovating entire rooms, but if I can save some shelf space by mounting my TV on the wall, you bet I'll do it.

Video Games

Video games used to be my favorite time waster. However, as I got older (and busier), there became much less time to waste. So, only occasionally you'll find me everlastingly-consumed by a power trip in Civilization 5 or the newest Doom release.
